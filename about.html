✦ OUR STORY

How It Started?

UP Govt Registered NGO
80G Certified
12A Registered

As we all know, 2020 was a year when the world was struggling through the COVID-19 pandemic. During this difficult period we felt a strong desire to contribute positively to society.

What began as a small effort by a few students slowly grew into a committed team of volunteers working together for community welfare.

On 28th March 2021, NayePankh Foundation officially started its journey with a mission to create opportunities, spread hope and empower communities.

NayePankh Foundation was established with a vision of empowering youth and creating meaningful social impact through education, community service, leadership and sustainable development. What started as a small initiative has grown into a dedicated organization committed to bringing positive change in society.
